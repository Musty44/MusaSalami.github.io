Research Philosophy

"I believe that the intersection of fundamental materials science and practical quantum device engineering holds the key to next-generation technologies. By understanding and controlling defects at the atomic scale, we can transform imperfections into functional quantum systems that advance computing, sensing, and communication."

Fun Fact: When I'm not in the lab characterizing quantum defects, I'm probably mentoring the next generation of physicists or contributing to open-source scientific computing projects!
